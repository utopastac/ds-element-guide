Buttons

Use buttons to move though states on systems and to action items. Ideally we would only use one button per page. They are designed to have maximum contrast and so always use the $theme-colour as their basis. Disabled buttons are set at 40% opacity.

Tabs

Segmented Control

Use a segmented control to switch between various states of a screen of related information or as a toggle button. Generally they can only have up to three options. The content directly below the control can be changed when an option is selected.
